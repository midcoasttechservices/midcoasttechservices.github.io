Kevin Smith's work reflects his love of nature, the Adirondack region of New York State, and New England. Although he once lived in the Adirondacks, Kevin ultimately made his home along the coast of Maine. He now finds his inspiration not only in the remotest areas of the Adirondack Park, but also in the beauty found along the east coast. A self-taught artist, he uses a pouring and blending technique.

Since 1998, Kevin's work has been featured in many prestigious shows throughout New York, has been published by a New England greeting card company, and is featured in the collection of a Boston art publishing company. His landscapes are vibrant, imaginative, and graphic.
